About Our College

Smt. K. B. Parekh College of Computer Science is dedicated to delivering high-quality education in computer science and information technology, fostering innovation, research, and practical skills to prepare students for the rapidly evolving digital world.

Vision

To become a center of excellence in computer education, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and lifelong learning, while empowering students to excel in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Mission

To empower students with advanced technical skills, ethical values, and a spirit of innovation, preparing them to face real-world challenges and contribute meaningfully to the global technology landscape.
