Admission Guide

Smt. K. B. Parekh College of Computer Science, Mahuva

Your colorful path to joining us – simple and easy!

Welcome! We're thrilled you're interested in applying. Follow this guide to complete your admission process smoothly.

How to Apply

  • Visit the College Official Website
  • Fill out the Online Application Form
  • Upload Required Documents
  • Submit Application & Pay Fees

Application Process

  1. Check Eligibility Criteria
  2. Submit Complete Application
  3. Application Review (7–10 Days)
  4. Receive Confirmation Email
  5. Pay Fees & Confirm Admission

Required Documents

  • Passport Size Photos
  • 10th & 12th Marksheet
  • Valid ID Proof / Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook Copy
  • Other Documents as Required

Note: Submit original documents with photocopies during verification.
